Interface & Status Glossary
Att Attended. Confirms student presence in the class session.
Late Late arrival. Requires inputting a specific context reason.
Abs Absent. Flagged context requires mandatory reason entry.
◷ Day Tardy. Flags the student as late/tardy for that specific calendar school day date.
⦸ Day Exclusion. Mutes the entire target column when the student is entirely absent from school for that specific date.
